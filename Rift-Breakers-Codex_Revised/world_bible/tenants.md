# The Tenants

1. Creation is intentional.
2. Reality was once unified.
3. The Cataclysm (also known as the Tear) fractured reality.
4. Actions have dual consequences.
5. Violence creates spiritual conflict.
6. Knowledge divides perception.
7. The Maker does not intervene directly.
8. Power requires permission or transgression.
9. Balance is not morality.
10. Truth cannot be destroyed.
