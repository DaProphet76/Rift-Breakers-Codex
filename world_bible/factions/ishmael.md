## Overview
A confederate nation defined by desert trade networks and population scale. Ishmael wields influence through logistics, routes, and numbers rather than elite military technology.

## Structure
- Caravan and route guilds as political power centers.
- Tribal trade councils integrated into confederate treaties.
- Mercantile militia units for route enforcement.

## Goals
- Control trade corridors and pricing through route dominance.
- Expand demographic leverage across regions.
- Maintain confederate alignment for protected trade access.

## Methods
- Route taxation and transit permissions.
- Contracted labor and bulk trade supply.
- Militia enforcement and escort services.

## Relationships
- **Edom:** primary treaty partner for trade legitimacy.
- **Canaan:** shared mercantile dependencies; hierarchical leverage.
- **Twelve Tribes:** utilized as labor pools and route-adjacent workforce.
