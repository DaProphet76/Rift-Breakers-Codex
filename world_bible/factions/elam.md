## Overview
A confederate nation defined by scientific and technological specialization. Elam provides research, systems engineering, and technical infrastructure that supports confederate control mechanisms.

## Structure
- Scholarium councils and research directorates.
- Applied engineering guilds.
- Confederacy contract offices for technical deployment.

## Goals
- Maintain confederate dependence on Elam systems.
- Advance internal research agendas through confederate funding.
- Control access to high-tier technology distribution.

## Methods
- Research monopolies and licensing.
- Technical maintenance contracts tied to political compliance.
- Controlled release of weapons and surveillance systems.

## Relationships
- **Edom/Amalek:** primary patrons and policy clients.
- **Moab/Ammon:** procurement relationships for war industry.
- **Twelve Tribes:** restricted access; used for labor and testing where permitted.
