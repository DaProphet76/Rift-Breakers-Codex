## Summary
Industrial Ruins are abandoned outer-ring factory complexes left to decay after the Sundering, now serving as the primary scavenging grounds for remnant technology.

## Description
The ruins consist of skeletal factory shells, collapsed production halls, rusted steel frameworks, and shattered windows that expose interiors to the elements. Catwalks hang twisted or broken above debris-choked floors. Old machinery lies frozen mid-operation, half-buried under dust, ash, and corrosion.

Light enters through torn roofs and blown-out walls, creating long shadows and deceptive sightlines. Sound carries unpredictably, echoing through hollow chambers and making it difficult to judge distance or movement.

## Environment / Conditions
- Structurally unstable floors and ceilings  
- Rusted beams and weakened supports  
- Wind-driven dust, ash, and debris  
- Intermittent power surges from dormant systems  
- Cold, damp interiors regardless of exterior weather  

## Population / Control
- **Who controls it:** No formal authority  
- **Who uses it:** Scavengers, small crews, occasional gangs  
- Control is informal and temporary, enforced by presence or violence  

## Risks
- Sudden structural collapse  
- Rival scavenger encounters  
- Gang ambushes or territory disputes  
- Exposure to toxic residue or unstable tech  
- Fires or electrical discharges from disturbed systems  

## Narrative Use
